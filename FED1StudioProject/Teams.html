NFL Ambassadors




Andy Reid

Andrew Walter Reid is an American football coach who is the head coach for the Kansas City Chiefs of the National Football League. Reid was previously the head coach of the Philadelphia Eagles, a position he held from 1999 to 2012. He has now coached in 3 SuperBowls and won 1 for the Kansas City Chiefs.




Patrick Mahomes

Patrick Lavon Mahomes II is an American football quarterback for the Kansas City Chiefs of the National Football League. He initially played college football and college baseball at Texas Tech University. By the young age of 25 he has already won a league MVP, a SuperBowl, and a SuperBowl MVP all with the Kansas City Chiefs.




Tyrann Mathieu

Tyrann Devine Mathieu, nicknamed "the Honey Badger" and "the Landlord", is an American football safety for the Kansas City Chiefs of the National Football League. He played college football for LSU. In college he developed a reputation for causing turnovers, setting a SEC record with 11 career forced fumbles and earning the nickname "Honey Badger." Since 2019 Tyrann has been a Kansas City Chief as well as a NFL Ambassador for the KC Football Academy.
